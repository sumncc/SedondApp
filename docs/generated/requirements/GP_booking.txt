GP Appointment Booking System
Version

1.0

1. Introduction
1.1 Purpose

The GP Appointment Booking System enables patients to book, modify, cancel, and manage appointments with General Practitioners (GPs). It also provides administrative and clinical functions for managing schedules, patient records, and appointment workflows.

The system should support web, mobile, and API clients.

2. Business Objectives
Reduce telephone booking workload.
Improve appointment utilisation.
Reduce missed appointments.
Provide self-service booking.
Improve patient satisfaction.
Support NHS-style appointment workflows.
Maintain complete audit history.
Ensure GDPR compliance.

6. Business Rules

BR-1

Patient cannot have two appointments at the same time.

BR-2

GP cannot exceed configured daily capacity.

BR-3

Appointments cannot be booked on holidays.

BR-4

Children under 16 require guardian details.

BR-5

Emergency appointments reserve 10% of slots.

BR-6

Maximum advance booking

90 days.

BR-7

Cancellation within 24 hours requires confirmation.
